LLAMA_B v3.0
Learning Words

  LLAMA_B tests how good you are at learning words in a foreign language.
  The test takes about 10 minutes.
  The program will give you two minutes to learn the names of 20 unfamiliar objects.
  Then it will give you a short test.

 

Use the orange buttons to enter your ID Code, then click BEGIN >>
